IMPACT Grant Project

Gerber/Hart Library & Archives

Website Redesign for Social Impact

3 Months
5 Team Members
Web Dev Focus Area

Project Overview

Amplifying LGBTQ+ voices through accessible digital design

This IMPACT grant project represents a collaborative effort with the Gerber/Hart Library & Archives to revolutionize their digital presence and strengthen their connection with Chicago's LGBTQ+ community.

Through comprehensive user research, UX design, and web development, our interdisciplinary team of software engineers, UX researchers, and designers focused on creating a more accessible and engaging platform. The primary goals included increasing website traffic, boosting donations, and enhancing the visibility of the library's invaluable LGBTQ+ historical resources.

By conducting targeted user interviews, creating interactive prototypes, and performing extensive usability testing, we delivered a user-centered digital experience that authentically represents Gerber/Hart's mission to preserve and share LGBTQ+ history and culture.

Final showcase presentation highlighting our research findings and design solutions

Project Features

Community Engagement

Increase website traffic and user engagement to better serve Chicago's LGBTQ+ community through improved digital presence.

Donation Growth

Streamline donation process and increase funding to support the library's mission and operations.

Resource Visibility

Enhance discoverability of LGBTQ+ historical archives and cultural resources for researchers and community members.

Accessibility Standards

Implement modern accessibility standards to ensure the website is usable by all community members.

Technical Challenges

User Research & Analysis

Conducting comprehensive interviews with library visitors and community members to understand diverse needs and create user personas that accurately represent the LGBTQ+ community.

Accessibility Implementation

Ensuring the website meets WCAG guidelines while maintaining visual appeal and implementing screen reader compatibility for all interactive elements.

Content Management System

Designing a user-friendly WordPress backend that allows library staff to easily update content, events, and resources without technical expertise.
