Project Overview

Amplifying LGBTQ+ voices through accessible digital design

This IMPACT grant project represents a collaborative effort with the Gerber/Hart Library & Archives to revolutionize their digital presence and strengthen their connection with Chicago's LGBTQ+ community.

Through comprehensive user research, UX design, and web development, our interdisciplinary team of software engineers, UX researchers, and designers focused on creating a more accessible and engaging platform. The primary goals included increasing website traffic, boosting donations, and enhancing the visibility of the library's invaluable LGBTQ+ historical resources.

By conducting targeted user interviews, creating interactive prototypes, and performing extensive usability testing, we delivered a user-centered digital experience that authentically represents Gerber/Hart's mission to preserve and share LGBTQ+ history and culture.
