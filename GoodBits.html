Get Off My Channel!

Interactive Visual Novel about Streaming Culture

3 Weeks
18 Team Members
Python Tech Stack

Project Overview

Exploring the fast-paced world of content creation

"Get Off My Channel!" is an interactive visual novel that dives into the chaotic and competitive world of online streaming. Players navigate the challenges of building a content creation career while managing fan expectations, dealing with critics, and handling unexpected events.

Developed using the Ren'Py engine, this project combines engaging storytelling with meaningful player choices that impact the narrative direction. The game explores themes of online fame, community building, and the personal toll of content creation.

Game Features

Interactive Storytelling

Dynamic narrative that adapts to player decisions, creating a personalized streaming journey experience.

Character Development

Rich character arcs with humorous dialogue and meaningful relationships in the streaming community.

Optimized Performance

Seamless gameplay experience with carefully optimized scripts and efficient resource management.

Community Building

Explore themes of online fame, community engagement, and the personal challenges of content creation.
