About the Project

"Get Off My Channel!" is an interactive visual novel developed in the Ren'Py engine, blending vibrant storytelling with dynamic gameplay. Players navigate the fast-paced world of streaming, juggling fans, critics, and unexpected events as they strive to grow their channel while managing daily challenges.

Features

  • Text-based storytelling with interactive gameplay choices
  • Humorous and engaging character arcs
  • Optimized gameplay scripts for seamless experience

Technologies Used

  • Python
  • Ren'Py Engine
