About the Project

Carbonbusters is a game created during the IndieCade Climate Jam. It focuses on transforming a car-dependent city by reducing transportation emissions and gaining political support to implement sustainable infrastructure changes, from adding bike lanes to replacing highways with rail lines.

Features

  • City transformation through political and infrastructural changes
  • Various transportation modes and their impact on emissions
  • Agent-based pathfinding between different infrastructure nodes
  • Real-time feedback on the city's environmental impact

Technologies Used

  • Godot (C#)
  • Graph data structures for pathfinding
  • Emissions management system
